Bloom Blog!

Hello! Welcome to the first ever Bloom Blog! On this blog page I will be giving you tips about your garden. So my first tip of the day is to remember to trim and prune your plants. You need to keep those leaves and branches under control. By trimming and pruning your plants, you are allowing your beloved plants to have increased air circulation. Another reason to prune your leaves is that there will not be as many leaves in the way of your routine watering. DO NOT WATER THE LEAVES. You want to be sure to water your plants, but by accidentally watering the leaves you are causing them to mold and rot. This leaves you with an unhappy, sick plant. You should be watering your plants with about an inch of water every week. The sign of an over watered plant is yellow leaves. Remember to be careful and have fun with your garden!
